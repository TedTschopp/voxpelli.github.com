About me and this site

So, my name is Pelle Wessman, but people might know me as VoxPelli on the web. I'm a swedish guy that currently lives in Malmö in southern Sweden, less than half an hour away from the danish capital of Copenhagen.

Since the autumn of 2010 I've been working on the micro-payment startup Flattr - first part time but in the spring of 2011 I quit my old job at the web agency Good Old to go almost full time at Flattr. I work there as a web developer, focusing mostly on the frontend parts.

During my time at Good Old I was part of building many Drupal and WordPress sites - both large and small. I also got the opportunity to contribute back to the Drupal community - posting issues, patches and our own modules. Those projects are stille alive, but I'm no longer a very active contributor to the Drupal community.

Prior to working at Good Old I was freelancing as a web developer from my home town Eslöv, helping designers turn their ideas into real web sites. During this time I was also active in a local concert organizer, "Alive", together with other youths - organizing among other things a one day festival, "Festival 0413", for three years in a row. One year even with a €10k grant from EU - which was quite a bureaucratic experience as I was organization's accounter at the time.
