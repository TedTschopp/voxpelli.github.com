About me and this site

So, my name is Pelle Wessman, but people might know me as VoxPelli on the web. I'm living in Malmö in southern Sweden, just besides the danish capital of Copenhagen.

I'm working as a web developer on blog reader startup Bloglovin.

Prior to that I worked as a consultant for Valtech Sweden, took part of the team behind micro-payment startup Flattr and grew up as a web developer in the arms of the Malmö web agency Good Old. And before and inbetween all of that there's been some philosophy studies, freelancing, non-profiting etc. – just to spice things up a bit.

As a web developer I've done tons of things – started out with vanilla PHP, went on to WordPress and Drupal and then Node.js took over – and then there's the frontend stuff as well! During my entire journey time I've got the opportunity to contribute back to both the Drupal community and the open source community in general (and the IndieWeb one in particular) - posting issues, patches and me and my colleagues own projects.
